MagicBlue shows all your incoming notifications from your multiple iPhones on your Mac. 

MagicBlue run with Bluetooth Low Energy, so don't worry about battery life.

■■■ Main Features ■■■

- Check notifications from multiple iPhones on your Mac
- Remove iPhone notification with Mac
- Sending text to your Mac with iPhone
- Bluetooth device discovery around


==== MagicBlue Compatible with ===

iPhone 4S or newer (with iOS 7 or newer)


Mac (with OSX 10.9 or newer)

Macbook Air 2011 or newer
Macbook pro 2012 or newer
iMac Late 2012 or newer
Mac mini 2011 or newer
Mac Pro Late 2013 or newer

==================================

To use MagicBlue app, you need to install MagicBlue for Mac.

you can download MagicBlue for Mac from http://hmhv.info/magicblue



MagicBlueで複数のiPhoneからの通知をあなたのMacでご確認できます。
